# Module-04-Challenge

This website includes a coding quiz that was made with the help of css, javascript, and index.html. Anyone can take this quiz to test there knowledge about coding. 

## Deployed Link

https://adorable-biscuit-0adc83.netlify.app/

## Landing Page

When you open the website you will have a description on how to take the quiz and what's the quiz about. It also gives you information about what if you answer any of the questions wrong. On top left hand corner there is a View high scores label and when you click on it you will see the high scores of people who have taken this quiz already. On top right hand corner there is timer to tell you how much time you have to take the quiz. On bottom there is a Start quiz button and once you click on it the quiz will start and the time will start to count down.

## Quiz Questions

Once you click start quiz button you will be presented with your first question and it will have four options for you to choose from and the timer will be active and it will be starting to count down.

<img width="1014" alt="Screen Shot 2022-10-05 at 3 50 11 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/112728880/194179708-c161eb69-27fd-48d1-a593-d2a0dc098123.png">

## Wrong Answer

Once you have answered your first question you will be given the feedback on if you have correctly answered the question and if you haven't there will be wrong written under the options and time will be taken out of the timer and the next question will be presented. 

<img width="1019" alt="Screen Shot 2022-10-05 at 3 50 46 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/112728880/194179937-fba7c985-6650-41ee-874b-cf8d69a0903b.png">

## Correct Answer

If you are able to answer the question correctly there will be correct written under the options and it will only stay there for a little time and then a new question will be presented and there won't be any time taken out of your timer. 

<img width="1024" alt="Screen Shot 2022-10-05 at 3 51 59 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/112728880/194180186-864c79fc-c69b-4315-89af-a9d0da414c9d.png">

## All Done!!

Once you are all done with the quiz you will be given your final socre and there will be a box given to you to enter your initials to save your scores and once you have typed in your initials you can click the submit button. 

<img width="1012" alt="Screen Shot 2022-10-05 at 3 51 29 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/112728880/194180871-6b029d22-e9a3-48a3-bc96-b91c909f00aa.png">

## View High Scores

Once you have submitted your scores then you can click on View High Scores to see your scores and your scores will be displayed there and after viewing your scores you could either choose to Go back or Clear All Scores on there.
